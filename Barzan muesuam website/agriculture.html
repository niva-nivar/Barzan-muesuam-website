The Agricultural Heritage of Barzan

Description

This exhibit offers a comprehensive look at the agricultural practices that have defined Barzan's rural life for centuries. Visitors can explore a wide array of historical farming tools, traditional irrigation methods, and detailed models of ancient farmsteads

Highlights:

  • Historical Tools: See a collection of plows, sickles, and threshers used by our ancestors.
  • Irrigation Systems: Learn about the ingenious irrigation techniques that have sustained our agriculture through dry seasons.
  • Traditional Farmstead: A meticulously reconstructed model of a traditional farm, complete with livestock areas and crop fields.

Educational Value: The exhibit educates visitors about sustainable farming practices, the evolution of agricultural technology, and the vital role of agriculture in the community’s development.
