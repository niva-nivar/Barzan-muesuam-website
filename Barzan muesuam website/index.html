Welcome to the Cultural Museum of Barzan

Nestled in the picturesque Baze village. Our museum is a beacon of heritage, offering an immersive journey into the rich tapestry of our region's history, culture, and natural beauty.

About us

Educational Programs and Community Engagement

At the Cultural Museum of Barzan, we believe in the power of education and community involvement to preserve and celebrate our rich heritage. Our educational programs and community engagement initiatives are designed to inspire curiosity, foster learning, and strengthen the bonds within our community.

Agriculture

Explore the agricultural practices that have sustained our community for centuries. From traditional farming tools to ancient irrigation techniques, our exhibits highlight the ingenuity and resilience of our ancestors.

Culture

Delve into the vibrant cultural practices that define Barzan. Our collection features traditional costumes, music instruments, and artifacts that tell the story of our community's rich cultural tapestry.

Learn More

Currency

Discover the evolution of trade and commerce in our region through our extensive collection of ancient coins and currency. Each piece offers a glimpse into the economic history and the various influences that have shaped our marketplace over the centuries.

Learn More

Documents

Our archive of historical documents provides invaluable insights into the political, social, and economic developments of Barzan. From ancient manuscripts to modern records, these documents are a treasure trove for historians and enthusiasts alike.

Learn More

Genocide

The museum also serves as a poignant reminder of the darker chapters of our history. Our genocide exhibit honors the memory of those who suffered and perished, ensuring that their stories are never forgotten and that we continue to learn from the past.

Learn More

Sedimentary Rock

Marvel at the geological wonders of our region with our collection of sedimentary rocks. These natural formations not only showcase the area's unique geology but also tell the story of the earth's ancient history, offering a tangible connection to the past.

About Teacher

At the Cultural Museum of Barzan, we are honored to have Dr. Anwar Hassan as our dedicated supervisor. With a profound passion for history and culture, Dr. Hassan brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to our museum, guiding our mission to preserve and celebrate the rich heritage of Barzan.
